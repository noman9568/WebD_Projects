Cucumber Cold Soup

Cucumber cold soup is refreshing, hydrating, and low in calories, making it an excellent summer dish. Packed with vitamins A and C, it supports skin health and boosts immunity.

RECIPE
Ingredient Used -
  1. 3 cucumber
  2. 1 cup chickpeas
  3. 1 handfuls coriander leaves
  4. black pepper as required
  5. 3 cloves garli
  6. 1 cup low fat yoghurt
  7. 2 green chilli
  8. garlic salt as required
  9. 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  10. water as required



How to make this recipe -
  1. Step 1 Peel the cucumber and cut them into small pieces To make this quick soup, wash and soak the chickpeas overnight, drain the water and pressure cook for 3-4 whistles. Wash and peel the cucumber and cut them into small pieces.
  2. Step 2 Blend it all Take a blender and add in the cucumber, green chilies, grated cucumber, chickpeas, yoghurt, black pepper, salt and coriander leaves. Keep blending till the mixture, turn into a thick creamy soup.
  3. Step 3 Add roasted garlic and enjoy Take a pan and add olive oil along with garlic, roast well and pour over the soup, give it a nice mix. Garnish the way you like and enjoy!
